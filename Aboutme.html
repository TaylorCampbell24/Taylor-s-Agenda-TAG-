Who Am I?

My name is Taylor Campbell. I am a Computer Science student at Georgia Tech. As you may have gathered, I am African American and have seen and experienced the causes of sexual assualt in my my life. When I was assigned this project, I knew that I had to do it on sexual assualt esspecially since I was created it during Sexual Assualt Awareness Month.

Although this website started as a school project, I do want to create it as something more in the future. I belive that the more people that are aware of the sexual assualt and its causes, the more that we can eliminate it.
