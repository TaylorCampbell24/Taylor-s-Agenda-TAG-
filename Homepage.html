How Culture Influences Sexual Violence Within Communities

Sexual Violence is defined as: to sexual activity/act when consent is not obtained or not given freely. There is also a rape culture that normalized sexual violence and exusess it through the use of misogynistic language, the objectification of women’s bodies, and the glamorization of sexual violence. A lot of the time, the individual components of rape culture stem from differnt cultures and communities with their own practices and norms that attempt to justify and allow sexual violence.
